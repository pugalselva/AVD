About Us

AVD Industrial Traders is managed by G. Govindha Vasan, M.Tech. (Proprietor) and is an authorised distributor for CUMI – Carborundum Universal Limited, part of the renowned Murugappa Group.

We supply a comprehensive range of premium abrasive products — from grinding wheels and cutting discs to polishing wheels and sanding belts — serving metal fabrication, automotive, construction, and engineering sectors across Villupuram & Tamil Nadu.
